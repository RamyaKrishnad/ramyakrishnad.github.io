About Me


I, Ramya Krishna Dhulipalla working as Assistant Professor in KLH,(Deemed to be University), Hyderabad and also Pursuing Ph.D in VIT-AP University. My Research domain is Deep Learning for Computer Vision, working in the area of "Applications of UAV in Precision Agriculture". One of the author in the research paper "Modified Yolo V3 for real-time Coconut Trees Detection from an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle".
