About Me


Ramya Krishna is doing her Ph.D in VIT-AP University. Also working as Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at VIT-AP from January 2021. She is currently doing her research work in the area Drones in Precison Agriculture. One of the author in the research paper "Modified Yolo V3 for real-time Coconut Trees Detection from an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle". She Worked at Vignan’s Nirula Institute Of Technology & Science For Women in the year (2012-2014). She Worked at Chalapathi Institute Of Technology from 2014 to 2020. she is having Lifetime Membership in International Association Of Engineers(IAENG) and International Society for Research and Development(ISRD).
